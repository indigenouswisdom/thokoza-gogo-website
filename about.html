About Us

Emalahleni Indigenous Wisdom NPC

Registered NPC (2021/802400/08), established to uplift and empower Traditional Health Practitioners (THPs) in Nkangala District, Mpumalanga.

We operate in Emalahleni, Victor Khanye, and Steve Tshwete municipalities with programmes rooted in African traditions, healing systems, and accreditation standards.
